Kofta


Description

Egyptian Kofta is a traditional Middle Eastern dish made from ground beef or lamb mixed with herbs and spices, then shaped into cylinders or patties and grilled to perfection. It's tender, flavorful, and perfect to serve with rice, bread, or salad.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Mix the ground meat with onion, parsley, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape the mixture into small cylinders or patties.
  3. Heat a grill pan or skillet with a little oil.
  4. Cook the kofta for 10–12 minutes, turning occasionally until browned and fully cooked.
  5. Serve hot with rice, bread, or salad.
