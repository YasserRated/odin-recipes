Pasta


Description

Pasta with Red Sauce is a simple and delicious Italian dish featuring tender pasta noodles tossed in a rich, flavorful tomato sauce. It's perfect for a quick dinner or a cozy meal at home, and can be garnished with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil and grated Parmesan for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper, and simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta and toss it with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
