Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Quick and colorful chicken stir fry with fresh vegetables, seasoned with soy sauce and garlic. Perfect for a fast, healthy dinner.

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken slices and cook until no longer pink.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Pour soy sauce, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 2 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
