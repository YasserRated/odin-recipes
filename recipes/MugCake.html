Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

A quick and easy chocolate mug cake made in the microwave in just a few minutes. Perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without baking an entire cake.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, and salt.
  2. Add milk, oil, and vanilla, stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 1–2 minutes, until the cake rises.
  4. Let it cool for a minute, then enjoy directly from the mug.
